Celebrating happiness

Immerse yourself in the grandeur of Consoulium, the annual literary festival meticulously organized under the auspices of Students Association of Bukhari Islamic da’wa College (Sabic). Rooted in a robust foundation, this distinguished event aims to redefine, stimulate, and reinvigorate the Islamic legacy of yore, while seamlessly incorporating contemporary elements and forward-looking intellectual insights. Within the sphere of arts and literary festivals lies a pivotal role in cultivating a repository of knowledge, shared widely within our diverse community. By creating platforms that facilitate exploratory exchanges among avid seekers of knowledge, these festivals ascend to become crucibles of insight. In the present landscape, educational and literary gatherings shoulder a weighty responsibility: to equip each participant with a transformative perspective. Upon the successful culmination of this event, attendees are poised to recalibrate their perception of pedagogy and learning. This newfound lens fosters a more incisive thought process, an astute response mechanism, and a heightened level of comprehension. Consoulium '24, a beacon of inspiration, is poised to instill within participants a rekindled commitment to both society and the global stage they inhabit. Join us in this enlightening odyssey where the synthesis of the past, present, and future births a profound metamorphosis within every participant.

Consoulium'24: The Happiness Festival

Consoulium'24 is the eagerly anticipated annual literary and cultural festival, meticulously crafted under the auspices of the Students’ Association of Bukhari Islamic Da'wa College (SABIC). This four-day festival is a cognitive explosion, showcasing the innate talents of students through a vibrant program of language conferences, lecture series, seminars, summits, exhibitions, book reviews, thought-provoking talks, and dynamic debates. Designed to be an immersive and transformative experience, Consoulium fosters a deeper understanding, encourages self-realization, and imparts timeless wisdom that participants carry with them long after the festival ends..
