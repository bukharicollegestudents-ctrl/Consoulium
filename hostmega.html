Bukhari Dawa College
to Host Mega Arts Festival Consolium '24

October 4, 2024 | Kondotty, Kerala

Bukhari Dawa College has announced its highly anticipated annual arts festival, Consolium '24, themed "The Festival of Happiness." Scheduled to take place from November 21 to 24, 2024, this vibrant celebration promises to bring together over 600 students from 13 campuses, showcasing their talents and fostering a sense of community.

Sheikhuna Abu Hanifal Faizi Thenala, General Secretary of Bukhari Group of Institutions, emphasized the importance of moral and cultural education in today's rapidly changing society during the festival's announcement ceremony. "We are moving through a time where moral consciousness is waning," Sheikhuna remarked. "Islam offers the correct answers to every crisis we face. Timely education is essential for moral and cultural progress."

Consolium '24 represents the culmination of 50 days of intense preparation, featuring various projects and training sessions organized by the Student Union SABIC. Participating teams will compete under colorful monikers such as HIRAHEIGHTS, LIQALEAVES, SIDRASIGHTS, SHAFALIGHTS, and HOUZEDEWS. The event will be presided over by Miqdad Baqavi Chunkathara, with notable attendees including Abdul Raouf Jouhari Alparamba, Kabir Bukhari Chirail, Abdul Aziz Saqafi Moothedam, Abdullah Bukhari Kuzhinjolam, Abdul Rasheed Bukhari Puliyakkod, and Swadikhali Bukhari Kolapuram.

The festival's announcement ceremony was a grand affair that set the tone for the upcoming extravaganza. Flags were handed over to group leaders Ansar Parappanangadi, Sinan CA Aloor, Nafih Kakkadampuram, Jabir Kollam, and Abdurahman Vadakkangara. As the countdown to Consolium '24 begins, students and faculty alike are gearing up for what promises to be a joyous celebration of arts, culture, and the pursuit of happiness. This festival not only showcases the talents of Bukhari Dawa College but also reinforces the importance of moral education and cultural awareness in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Student Union SABIC Leads Intense Preparations forConsoulium ’24
  • • October 4, 2024

Kondotty, Kerala: The Student Union SABIC at Bukhari Dawa College is in the final stages of preparation forConsoulium ’24, an arts festival themed “The Festival of Happiness.” Over fifty days of intense projects and training sessions have culminated in a grand event that will feature more than 600 students from 13 campuses. The festival, set to take place from November 21 to 24, 2024, aims to showcase student talents and foster a sense of community, with teams competing under joyful names like HIRAHEIGHTS and LIQALEAVES.

Consoulium'24 Logo Unveiled: A Vibrant Representation of Happiness
  • • October 4, 2024

Kondotty, Kerala: The much-anticipated Happiness Festival logo was officially unveiled by Al-Usthad Aboo Haneefal Faizy Thennala, capturing the vibrant essence of this year’s theme: Happiness. The logo symbolizes the festival’s commitment to promoting joy, spiritual elevation, and unity through its diverse events. The launch marked an inspiring beginning for Consoulium'24, setting the tone for the celebrations ahead.

Consoulium'24 Theme Talk 01: A Call to Contemplation and Action
  • • October 4, 2024

Kondotty, Kerala: The first Theme Talk of Consoulium'24, titled "Knowledge, Experience, and joy", was delivered by Usthad Abdul Vasih Baqavi Kuttippuram. Drawing from his personal journey of knowledge hunting and teaching, he shared inspiring incidents from his life and reflections on studying Islamic traditional Kithabs. He also highlighted the dedication and sacrifices of previous scholars and Ustads, showcasing their profound methods of imparting knowledge and their unwavering commitment to the field of learning which was a matter of joy for them. The talk resonated deeply with the audience, setting a contemplative tone for the festival's thematic explorations.

Sayyid Ibrahim Khaleel Al Bukhari Unlocks Consoulium'24 Official Website
  • • October 4, 2024

Kondotty, Kerala: The official Consoulium’24 website - www.consoulium.com - was launched by Sayyid Ibrahim Khaleel Al Bukhari in a grand ceremony. Designed as a one-stop hub, the website offers detailed event information, live updates, galleries, competition results, and news. This digital platform ensures accessibility and engagement, connecting participants and audiences across campuses with the spirit of the festival.

Consoulium'24 Offstage: Talent Meets Innovation
  • • November 4, 2024

Kondotty, Kerala: A variety of offstage competitions were held, offering students the opportunity to showcase their talents across multiple genres. Events ranged from retention and religious mastery to cognition, creative writing, illustration, and technical challenges, blending tradition with innovation. These mind-setting activities not only tested participants’ skills but also encouraged holistic development, aligning perfectly with the festival’s mission of fostering intellectual and spiritual growth.
